Regional Highlights

Key historical regions of India

North India

Cradle of Empires

Home to the Indus Valley Civilization, Maurya and Gupta empires, and the Mughal heartland. Contains Delhi, Agra, and Varanasi - cities that shaped Indian history.

Delhi Agra Varanasi

South India

Temple Kingdoms

Home to ancient Dravidian kingdoms like Chola, Pandya, and Vijayanagara. Known for magnificent temples, rich literature, and maritime trade.

Chola Temples Trade

West India

Maratha Power

The land of Shivaji Maharaj and the Maratha Empire. Also home to ancient port cities like Lothal and the rock-cut caves of Ajanta and Ellora.

Maratha Caves Ports

East India

Cultural Heritage

Center of Bengal Renaissance and India's freedom movement. Home to ancient universities like Nalanda and Vikramshila.

Bengal Nalanda Renaissance
